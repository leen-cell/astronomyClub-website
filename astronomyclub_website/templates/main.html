The establishment

The Birzeit University Astronomy Club, founded in 2015, promotes astronomy knowledge within the university and across Palestine. It organizes stargazing events, astronomy courses, and public lectures, while collaborating with local and international astronomy institutions. The club is equipped with advanced telescopes and tools. You may want to make a little donation to the astronomy club to continue our researches and stargazing events we organize!

Location

The Birzeit University Astronomy Club is located on the 4th floor of the Faculty of Education building. Here, you'll find the club's observatory—a defining feature of our identity. Its presence is not just practical but symbolic, standing out as a beacon of exploration and curiosity. The observatory embodies our passion for stargazing and serves as a hub for our astronomical activities, making it an unmistakable part of our club's character.
